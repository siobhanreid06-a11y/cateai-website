Our Story

I built the tool I always wished existed.

After more than twenty years working in People & Culture — across regulated sectors, small organisations, and governance-heavy environments — I kept running into the same problem.

Leadership teams were making decisions about their people without any real data. And the HR Managers and Directors supporting them were spending hours every month manually pulling figures together from multiple systems, just to produce a report that rarely showed the full picture. Not because anyone didn't care — they absolutely did — but because the tools that could help were built for enterprises, priced for enterprises, and required an IT procurement process most organisations simply couldn't navigate.

At the same time, staff were accessing policies from shared drives nobody had updated in three years, finding out about their benefits from a colleague in the kitchen, and completing onboarding that amounted to a folder of PDFs. There was no single place for people to feel connected to their organisation.

CateAI Culture Pulse™ is my answer to both problems. A monthly dashboard that gives leadership teams the people data they should have been seeing all along — and a staff engagement hub that makes every employee feel genuinely informed, supported, and connected. Joined by a pulse survey that makes staff voice a leadership metric.

CateAI is not a software product. It is an expert-managed service — delivered by a qualified People & Culture professional who analyses every data point, shapes every narrative, and stands behind every insight. The judgement, the language, and the professional read of your organisation comes from real HR experience. Because data without interpretation is just noise.

Siobhan Reid

Privacy & Cookie Policy

Who we are

CateAI Culture Pulse™ is a People & Culture analytics service operated by Siobhan Reid, trading as CateAI, based in Ireland. Contact: hello@cateai.ie

What data we collect

When you contact us or book a demo, we collect your name and email address. If you are a client, we process anonymised employee HR data on your behalf under a signed Data Processing Agreement. We do not collect data through this website beyond contact enquiries.

How we use your data

Contact data is used solely to respond to your enquiry. Client HR data is used only to produce the agreed monthly dashboard and People Hub. We do not sell, share or use data for any other purpose.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking tools. The only cookies that may be set are strictly necessary technical cookies required for the website to function.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@cateai.ie.

Data retention

Raw client HR data is deleted within 7 days of dashboard delivery. Contact enquiry data is retained for the duration of our business relationship plus 2 years.

Questions

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@cateai.ie. If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you may contact the Data Protection Commission at dataprotection.ie.

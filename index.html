Our Story

Professional Judgement
Meets AI Speed.

CateAI was founded to solve a specific problem — small and mid-sized organisations were flying blind because enterprise tools were too complex and too expensive.

The Innovation. We use advanced AI to find the patterns in your people data — and professional judgement to find the story. That combination is what separates a CateAI dashboard from a spreadsheet report.

The Hybrid Approach. People data is only useful when professional judgement sits behind it. Our AI identifies the signals. Our People & Culture expertise interprets them. You get clarity — not just numbers.

Our Mission. To give every CEO and HR Manager a digital analyst that delivers clarity on demand — regardless of what internal systems they use. No new software. No IT project. Just your data, transformed.

How It Works

The CateAI Culture Pulse™ Process

Four steps from raw data to executive insight.

📤
Step 1

Secure Data Transfer

You send your data using our simple CateAI Data Template. Whether you use a modern HR system or basic spreadsheets — we handle it.

🤖
Step 2

AI Processing

Our AI-powered process identifies patterns in absence, hours, and culture health that manual reporting consistently misses.

✍️
Step 3

Professional Narrative

Every AI-generated report is reviewed and narrated by a qualified People & Culture professional to ensure actionable, accurate insight.

📊
Step 4

Live Dashboard

Your dashboard updates at your private, secure URL — giving your leadership team the "so what?" behind the numbers, every month.
